# LinkedIn Project Summary

## Short Post Draft

I built a practical phishing analysis workflow as part of my transition from IT support into junior cybersecurity operations.

The project focuses on the basics that matter in real triage:

- receiving a user-reported suspicious email
- preserving evidence
- reviewing sender, reply-to, headers, links, and attachments
- extracting indicators of compromise
- checking reputation safely
- classifying risk
- recommending containment
- writing clear incident notes

The main lesson: phishing analysis is not just spotting a suspicious email. It is documenting the evidence clearly enough that another analyst can understand what happened and what should happen next.
